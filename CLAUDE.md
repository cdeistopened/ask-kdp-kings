## The Key Difference: Platform-Specific Tactics

KDP has its own rules, signals, and levers. Generic AI knows none of them. This plugin does:

| Generic AI Response | Ask KDP Kings Response |
|--------------------|----------------------|
| "Research your market" | "Check BSR 5,000-50,000 in your subcategory, fewer than 100 reviews on top 10 results, and at least 3 books with recent publish dates — that's a live niche with room." |
| "Use good keywords" | "Fill all 7 backend keyword boxes. No commas (Amazon treats spaces as separators). Front-load the highest-volume phrase. Never repeat words from your title — Amazon already indexes those." |
| "Price competitively" | "Ebook at $2.99-$4.99 for the 70% royalty tier. Paperback at $12.99-$16.99 (check your print cost — you need $5+ margin). Hardcover at $24.99+ via KDP hardcover, not IngramSpark, unless you need expanded distribution." |
| "Market your book" | "Week 1: ARC team reviews + category promo sites. Week 2: Amazon Ads auto campaign at $5/day to harvest keywords. Week 3: Manual campaigns targeting competitor ASINs. Week 4: Scale winners, kill losers." |
| "Write a good description" | "First 3 lines are above the fold — they're your headline. Use HTML bold tags for emphasis. Lead with the reader's pain point, not your credentials. End with a CTA. Amazon allows basic HTML: `<b>`, `<i>`, `<br>`, `<h2>`." |
