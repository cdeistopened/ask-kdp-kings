## When to Use

Use this checklist every time you upload a new title to KDP -- ebook, paperback, or hardcover. Follow it field by field to avoid the mistakes that waste hours, harm discoverability, or lock you into irreversible decisions.

## The Framework

### Pre-Upload: Prepare Everything First

Do not log into KDP until every asset is in a folder and ready to paste.

**Account prerequisites:**
- [ ] KDP account created at kdp.amazon.com
- [ ] Tax information submitted (W-9 for US, W-8BEN for international)
- [ ] Bank account linked for royalty deposits

**Ebook asset checklist:**
- [ ] Manuscript file (ePub preferred; DOCX and KPF accepted)
- [ ] Front cover image (JPEG or TIFF, minimum 2,560 x 1,600 pixels)
- [ ] Book description with HTML formatting (up to 3,000 characters)
- [ ] 7 keyword phrases (from keyword research)
- [ ] 1-3 category selections identified and verified (not ghost categories)
- [ ] Metadata document with title, subtitle, author name, description ready to copy-paste

**Paperback asset checklist (in addition to above):**
- [ ] Interior manuscript as PDF
- [ ] Full wrap cover as PDF (front, spine, back)
- [ ] Trim size selected

**Hardcover asset checklist:**
- [ ] Same interior as paperback (reuse works)
- [ ] Different cover dimensions than paperback (PDF, full wrap)
- [ ] Separate ISBN (free KDP or purchased)

### The Three Irreversible Decisions

These cannot be changed after publishing. Decide carefully before you click.

**1. Digital Rights Management (DRM)**

"Digital Rights Management is supposed to protect your ebook against piracy. I don't recommend this option but feel free to research if it makes sense for you. Once you select this option you cannot undo it later." -- Dale L Roberts

Consensus: skip DRM. The marginal piracy protection is not worth the friction.

**2. KDP Select Enrollment**

Locks your ebook into a 90-day exclusive agreement with Amazon. Grants access to Kindle Unlimited, Countdown Deals, and Free Promotions. Auto-renews unless you opt out before the period ends.

"If you plan on publishing elsewhere you will want to leave that deselected, because when you select it you are telling Amazon this ebook will not be published anywhere during the course of the 90-day agreement." -- Dale L Roberts

Working rule: fiction titles default to KDP Select; nonfiction titles consider going wide from the start.

**3. ISBN Choice**

For ebooks, ISBN is optional -- Amazon assigns a free ASIN. For print, you choose between a free KDP ISBN (publisher listed as "Independently Published") or a purchased Bowker ISBN (your company name as publisher). Purchased ISBNs cost $125 for a single or less in bulk.

"You don't have to [enter] an ISBN because Amazon will give you a free ISBN." -- Sean Dollwet

### Ebook Upload: Field by Field

**Page 1 -- Book Details:**
- Language, title, subtitle (keyword-informed)
- Series designation if applicable
- Author and contributors
- Description with HTML formatting: "If you just copy and paste your words into this, your book description will look like a giant block of text." -- Dave Chesson
- Publishing rights, primary audience, reading age (children's only)
- Categories (3 picks -- verify none are ghost categories)
- Keywords (7 boxes, 50 characters each)
- Pre-order vs. immediate release

**Page 2 -- Content:**
- DRM decision (irreversible)
- Manuscript upload (ePub recommended)
- Cover upload
- AI-generated content disclosure
- Launch Previewer -- always check across device views
- ISBN (optional for ebook)

**Page 3 -- Pricing:**
- KDP Select enrollment (irreversible for 90 days)
- Territories (select all unless rights are sold regionally)
- Royalty rate: 70% ($2.99-$9.99) or 35% (outside that range)
- Book lending toggle

### Paperback Upload: What Changes

KDP carries over metadata from your ebook. Additional decisions:
- Print ISBN (free KDP or purchased Bowker)
- Paper type (white or cream)
- Ink type (black-and-white, standard color, premium color)
- Bleed settings
- Cover upload (must be PDF, full wrap)
- Expanded distribution (turn OFF if using D2D or IngramSpark for wide print)

"For expanded distribution... I normally don't check this box because even though your book is listed on more retailers and more websites, the royalty is significantly lower." -- Sean Dollwet

### Hardcover Upload: What Changes

- Separate ISBN required (cannot share with paperback)
- Different cover dimensions (case wrap only on KDP)
- No expanded distribution available for hardcover

### Post-Upload

- Review takes up to 72 hours (ebooks often go live within hours)
- Check your live listing for truncated subtitles, category accuracy, and description formatting
- Verify "Look Inside" works correctly once enabled (may take 24-48 hours)

## Example

First-time publisher uploading a self-help ebook and paperback:

1. Prepared metadata document with title, subtitle, description (HTML), 7 keywords, 3 categories
2. Ebook: ePub file, JPEG cover, DRM declined, KDP Select declined (nonfiction), $4.99 at 70% royalty
3. Paperback: PDF interior, PDF wrap cover, 6x9 trim, white paper, black-and-white ink, free KDP ISBN, $12.99
4. Previewer checked on phone, tablet, and Kindle views
5. Published both formats -- linked automatically by KDP

## Output

A completed upload produces:
- Live ebook listing with verified categories and formatted description
- Live paperback listing with matched metadata
- All three irreversible decisions documented
- Metadata document saved for future format uploads (hardcover, audiobook)
- Post-launch verification checklist completed
