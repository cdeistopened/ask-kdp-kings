## When to Use

Use this workflow when you are ready to outsource book writing -- whether you are commissioning your first ghostwritten title or building a team of writers for catalog scaling. Also use the plagiarism check protocol for every manuscript you receive from any external writer.

## The Framework

### Choose Your Hiring Path

**Path 1: Ghostwriting Companies (Best for Beginners)**

Start here if you have never published a book. The purpose is calibration -- learning what separates a good manuscript from a bad one.

"In the beginning use a ghost writing company to save time and also to learn the publishing process and most importantly you got to learn the difference between what makes a good quality book and a bad quality book." -- Sean Dollwet

**Company Rate Benchmarks (per 100 words):**

| Company | Rate Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| The Urban Writers | $1.99-$2.89 | Sean's top pick; Upwork-like writer selection |
| The Writing Summit | $0.99-$2.75 | Long track record since 2016; does Spanish translations |
| eWriter Solutions | $1.50-$2.00 | Cheapest; inconsistent quality |
| Epic Write | $1.40 | Best quality at lowest price, 1-5 month delivery |
| Emporium Writers | $2.70 | Good customer service; newer company |

Always order from the top two tiers. Quality drops sharply at the cheapest packages.

**The 5-Criteria Scorecard:**
1. **Longevity** -- operating for years
2. **Track record** -- consistent delivery history
3. **Customer support** -- responsive communication
4. **Writing quality** -- actual output quality, not marketing promises
5. **Continuous improvement** -- getting better over time

**Path 2: Upwork Direct Hiring (Best for Long-Term Quality and Cost)**

"Ghost writing companies they all hire from up work or those kind of sites as well and they just mark it up a little bit." -- Sean Dollwet

**Method A: Search for Established Talent**
1. Search "Ghostwriter" plus your niche keywords
2. Filter: freelancers only (not agencies), 90%+ job success, $10K+ earned, 100-1,000 hours billed, native/bilingual English
3. Ignore country of origin -- focus on reviews and samples

"Some of my top writers that got literally 4.7 4.8 average reviews across hundreds of reviews... one of them was from Nigeria another one was from Serbia." -- Sean Dollwet

**Method B: Post a Job to Attract Applicants**
- Title: "Expert Ghost Writer for [Niche] -- Long Term"
- Budget: $1,000-$10,000 (catches attention; actual rate goes in description)
- Include: niche/topics, personal experience requirement, delivery timeline, rate per 100 words, sample request, and a passphrase test

"Please apply with the phrase pink elephant just to make sure that they read until the end of the post." -- Sean Dollwet

**The Two-Number Rule:** Never evaluate on job success percentage alone. Check both the percentage (90%+) AND total earned ($5K-$10K minimum).

### Negotiate and Structure Payment

Promise ongoing work for bulk discounts. Require milestone payments at 10,000-word intervals -- this protects you if quality drops mid-project.

**Upwork Rate Benchmarks (per 100 words):**

| Category | Rate Range | Typical Book Cost (30K words) |
|---|---|---|
| Medium content (trivia, riddles) | $2.00 | $200-$300 |
| General nonfiction | $2.20-$2.50 | $660-$750 |
| Technical topics | $2.50-$3.00+ | $750-$900+ |

### The Two-Step Plagiarism Check (Do This for Every Book)

**Step 1: Check for encoding.** Scammers insert invisible characters between letters to defeat plagiarism checkers. Warning signs: Word shows red underlines on correctly-spelled words; speech-to-text reads as garbled nonsense.

**Step 2: Check for standard plagiarism.** Only after encoding test passes, run through Grammarly or Copyscape.

"If you get busted you're 100% going to be losing your Amazon KDP account." -- Sean Dollwet

### Decision Matrix

| Your Situation | Use | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Brand new to publishing | Ghostwriting company | Learn good vs. bad quality |
| Testing unproven keyword | Cheapest company tier | Minimize risk |
| Proven keyword, want lasting quality | Company top tier or Upwork | Invest for long-term earnings |
| Want lowest cost, willing to invest time | Upwork directly | Cut out the middleman |
| Need consistent voice across books | Same Upwork writer long-term | Same writer = same voice |
| Many books simultaneously | Ghostwriting companies | Managing many freelancers is hard |

## Example

Publisher scaling from 3 to 15 titles:
1. First 3 books: The Urban Writers (top tier, $2.89/100 words) to learn quality benchmarks
2. Books 4-8: Post Upwork job, hire 2 writers at $2.30/100 words with passphrase filter
3. Books 9-15: Keep best Upwork writer at negotiated $2.00/100 words (bulk discount for ongoing work)
4. Every manuscript: encoding check first, then Grammarly plagiarism scan

## Output

A completed hiring workflow produces:
- Selected hiring path (company or Upwork) with rationale
- Vetted writer or company with scorecard evaluation
- Milestone-based payment structure
- Two-step plagiarism check results for every delivered manuscript
- Rate benchmarks documented for future negotiations
